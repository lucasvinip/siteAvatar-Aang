Yellows

The characters of "The Simpsons" are not yellow by chance, according to their creator, Matt Groening. "When it came time to choose the color, I didn't want conventional cartoon colors. An animator came out with the yellow Simpsons and as soon as she showed it to me I said, 'This is the answer!'

In family

The names of the main characters are inspired by Matt Groening's own family. His parents were Homer Groening (1919-1996) and Margaret Wiggum (1919-2013), who inspired the name Marjorie Simpson, Marge. Groening also has sisters named Lisa Groening and Margaret Groening, or Maggie.
